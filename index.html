Welcome to our tiny-house, or as we call it, "The Pod"! This compact yet modern house prefectly accommodates 2 people and offers you everything you need for a comfortable stay within the natural landscapes of Ballyconnell, North Sligo. The house inlcudes all neccessities for your holiday stay. The local area is surrounded by beautiful nature and an array of outdoor scenery and activties including swim-friendly sandy beaches, over 10 different walking/hiking trails, three renound surf spots, various fishing spots, cycling trails and more!
